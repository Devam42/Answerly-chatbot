Answerly: One Bot. Infinite Sources. Unmatched Insights.

How to Use

  1. Enter Your Username: Provide your desired username in the designated input field.
  2. Add Resources: Include up to three of each type of resource:
    • YouTube Links
    • Website URLs
    • Wikipedia Articles
    • Files
    • Audio
    • Video
  3. Ask a Question: After adding your resources, type your question related to them in the question input field.
  4. Interact with the Bot: Engage in a conversation with the bot about your provided resources.
  5. End Conversation (Optional): Click the "End" button to terminate the conversation at any time.

Note: The bot may take a moment to provide the first answer or summary while it processes your resources. Please be patient. Once it has read your files, subsequent answers will come faster.
