How to Use

Enter your username, then include up to three of each type of resource (YouTube links, websites, Wikipedia articles, files, audio, or video). After that, ask your question about those resources, and you'll be able to chat with the bot about them. Note: The bot may take a moment to provide the first answer or summary while it processes your resources. Please be patient. Once it has read your files, subsequent answers will come faster.
